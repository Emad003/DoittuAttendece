How It Works

Mark your attendance in under 5 seconds — no cards, no signatures, just your face.

1

Register Your Account

Sign up with your university email, verify with OTP, and capture your face during registration. Takes 2 minutes.

2

Scan Your Face

Open the attendance page, select your subject, and look at the camera. AI matches your face in under 2 seconds.

3

Attendance Marked

Your attendance is instantly saved to the system. Faculty can view it live on the admin dashboard.

Features

Everything You Need

A complete attendance management solution built for modern universities.

Face Recognition

AI-powered face detection runs entirely in the browser. No photos stored — only mathematical face data.

Email Verification

Every student must verify their email with OTP before registering. No fake accounts.

Duplicate Prevention

System automatically prevents marking attendance twice for the same subject on the same day.

Live Dashboard

Faculty get a real-time admin panel with today's attendance, student list, and subject-wise stats.

Low Attendance Alerts

Automatic email alerts sent to students whose attendance drops below 75%.

Export Reports

Download attendance records as CSV for any date range, subject, or department.
